Submit a case to the Cascade Archives

Submit your Case to the Cascade Archives and other players can sign up to play it! Each time your case is completed by other players, you receieve 1/2 of the points the players spent added directly to your balance. That means you can play Cascade for free by contributing quality content to it!

Here are the rules:

  • You need to play 5 cases before you submit a case.
  • Make sure you need information from each investigator to solve the case. Do not make any participants' role meaningless.
  • No profanity, racism or intolerance.
  • No extreme violence. It can be a murder case, but don't go into the details!
  • No politics unless it's over 200 years old.

Cases will be reviewed for approved content before they become available.
